About Us

KetoLife Café was born from a shared passion for healthy living and delicious food. In the beautiful city of Zurich, four extraordinary women—Dipti, Svetlana, Valerie, and Zineb—came together with a common vision: to create a dining experience that proves healthy eating doesn't mean sacrificing flavor.

Each founder brings her unique cultural background and culinary expertise to our kitchen. Dipti's vibrant Indian flavors, Svetlana's European elegance, Valerie's Swiss precision, and Zineb's Mediterranean warmth blend together to create a truly international keto menu.

We believe that the ketogenic lifestyle should be accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable. Every dish at KetoLife Café is carefully crafted using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, ensuring that you receive not only a meal, but an experience that nourishes both body and soul.
